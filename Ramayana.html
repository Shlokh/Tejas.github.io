Ramayana The Epic

The Ramayana written by Tulsidas known as Ramcharitmanas,is a sixteenth century epic.The story revolves around Rama,who was the seventh avtar of the Hindu God Vishnu.He is the central figure of the Hindu epic Ramayana. which is the principal narration of the events connected to his incarnations on earth,his ideal and his greatness.he was the oldest son of King Dashrath the ruler of Kosala Kingdom.Rams life and journey is one of adherence to dharama despite harsh tests and obstacles and many pains of life and time.This adherence made him earn the name of Maryada Purushottam means Perfect Man or Lord of Self-control or Lord of virtue.For the sake of his father honours,Rama abandones his claim to Ayodhya's throne to serve an exile of fourteen years in the forest.His wife Sita and brother Lakshmana decided to join him,and all three spend the fourteen years in exile together.When in exile,Sita kidnapped by Ravana,king of Lanka.After a long and anduous search,Rama fight a clossal war against Ravana armies.In war of powerful and magical beings, greatly destructive weponary and battles,Rama slays in battle and liberates his wife,Having completed his exile,Rama returns to be crowned king in ayodhya and eventually become the emperor.The whole story is divided into seven parts known as "Kands"
